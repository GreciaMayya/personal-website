Birds of Sikkim

Himalayan bulbul biodiversity

Used public data from eBird to map changes in the number of observations reported to eBird as a proxy for capturing seasonal geographic changes of the Himalayan bulbul in Sikkim.

To create the map and video visualization, I used QGIS and Canva.

I presented this work at the Society for Conservation GIS conference in July 2026 in Estes Park, Colorado.

Sikkim black-throated tit

Used QGIS to create a clean map of Sikkim, a northern state of India where Black-throated tits can be found year-round.

Mountain hawk-eagle

Used QGIS to process and visualize elevation data across the state of Sikkim. This northern Indian state is home to the Mountain-hawk eagle, a bird of prey with a signature crest of elongated feathers.

Featured GIS project

Cambridge cherry tree bike map

Open full map

I mapped cherry blossom trees across City of Cambridge to make them easier to explore by bike. You can zoom in and explore different cherry blossom clusters to create your own bike route this spring or maybe plan for next year! 🌸

A big thank you to Spatial Thoughts for leading an incredible course on Python and spatial analysis, where I gained confidence working with tools like geopandas and APIs.

Data sources:

  • 🌳 City of Cambridge Street Trees
  • 🚲 City of Cambridge bike facilities
