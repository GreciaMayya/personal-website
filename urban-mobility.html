Spatial justice research

Bus Stops and Perceived Safety Through a Gender Lens
Daytime vs Nighttime Differences in Feelings of Safety Along MBTA Bus Route 1

Through this independent research project, I designed and distributed a bus rider survey to collect data on percevied safety along bus route 1, extending from the Roxbury neighborhood in Boston to Harvard Square in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

I coupled the survey results data with additional data sources including MBTA's dataset on bus stop amenities and used ArcGIS to map and analyse the data.

I had the opportunity to present my findings to the Boston Region Metropolitan Planning Organization's Transit Working Group.

This work was also featured in the 2022 State of Transportation Planning Report of the American Planning Association’s Transportation Division.

Thank you to the Congress for New Urbanism New England Chapter for sponsoring this body of research through the Spatial Justice Fellowship.
